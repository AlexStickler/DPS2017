HAYES FUNERAL HOME LEDGERS

(1902) - (1950)

The Hayes Ledgers are a collection of funeral documents from the Hayes Cemetery which is
the first African American owned Business in the Greater Memphis Area

  • Features:


  • Search

    A classic search tool: type in what you want to search, and you'll be taken to a table of all relevant results. Clicking on a name in the table will take you to their ledger. If you click on the first name it will bring you to a picture of the ledger!


  • Map

    A map tool: enter information into the fields that you would like to filter by and results will be returned with points on the map of people's locations that match the input.
