Features


  • Search

    A classic search tool; type in what you want to search, and you'll be taken to a table of all relevant results. Clicking on an individual's name in the table will take you to their ledger.

  • Browse

    Our complete collection of ledger scans, organized into volumes. Click on a decade to be taken to its corresponding volume.

  • Explore

    Analytic tools such as frequency distributions, bar graphs, and line graphs.

  • Map

    An ineractive Google map that allows you to see ledger data in the context of its geographic loction.
